About us

And where we get inspiration from

We meet each other in Chicago, the place where we found ourselves in ceramics. Inspired by nature, the natural elements like dirt, rocks, and sand, architecture both ancient and modern, our work has minimalist shapes and the raw materials bare so we never forget what they are made of.

You can be sure that we make pottery with a more meaningful story than ceramics that have been mass produced.
